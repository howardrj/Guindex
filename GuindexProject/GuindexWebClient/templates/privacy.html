For purposes of this Agreement, “Site” refers to the Guindex's website, which can be accessed at www.guindex.ie. “Service” refers to the Company’s services accessed via the Site, in which users can submit their observed prices of pints of Guinness in public houses and on-licensed facilities in Dublin city and county. Users can also inform us about whether the aforementioned establishments are failing to offer Guinness on draught or whether any permanent changes to the ongoing operation of these establishments have occurred in the time since the last user has visited the establishment. The terms “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to the Company. “You” refers to you, as a user of our Site or our Service.
